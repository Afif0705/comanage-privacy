CoManage Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the CoManage app for mobile devices and any related services operated by PalNova Innovations LLC, collectively referred to as the "Application." PalNova Innovations LLC is referred to in this Privacy Policy as the "Service Provider."

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application may collect and store information that users voluntarily provide when creating an account, signing in, managing a workspace, contacting support, or using features within the Application. This may include information such as an email address, phone number, profile name, household or workspace information, tasks, bills, appointments, budget items, supplies, documents, insurance records, uploaded files, notes, and other information entered by the user.

This information is used to provide the Application's features, allow users to access their account, sync data across devices when applicable, support workspace functionality, improve the user experience, respond to support requests, and maintain the security and functionality of the Application.

Account and authentication information

The Application may use third-party services, including Firebase Authentication, to allow users to create accounts, sign in, verify email addresses, verify phone numbers, reset passwords, and protect account access. Authentication information may be processed by these services as necessary to provide secure account functionality.

Uploaded files, documents, and user content

Users may choose to upload or save documents, photos, notes, receipts, insurance files, or other records inside the Application. Users are responsible for deciding what information they choose to add. The Service Provider recommends avoiding the upload or storage of highly sensitive information unless necessary.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information?

The Application does not collect precise real-time location information from your mobile device or computer.

Do third parties see or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Application may use trusted third-party service providers to operate core features, such as authentication, cloud storage, database syncing, app hosting, analytics, crash reporting, or support communication. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services to the Application.

The Service Provider does not sell users' personal information.

What are my opt-out rights?

Users may stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application. Users may also request deletion of account-related information or support communications by contacting the Service Provider at support@palnovainnovations.com.

Data retention

The Service Provider retains user-provided information for as long as necessary to provide the Application's services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security, and operate the Application. Users may request deletion of their data by contacting support@palnovainnovations.com.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age, or such higher age as required by applicable law. The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit personal information from children or knowingly market to children. If the Service Provider becomes aware that a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, reasonable steps will be taken to delete that information.

Security

The Service Provider takes reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. The Service Provider cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Application after changes are posted means users accept the updated Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2026-06-04.
